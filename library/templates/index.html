Welcome

This application is designed to test if the Watson artifical intelligence engine can predict the future condition of a given market based on news articles.
We make the following assumptions:

  • There are only four countries in the world:
    • Eurasia
    • Eastasia
    • Oceana
    • Mundania
  • Conflict between these nations will:
    • Limit supply in the short term
    • Reduce demand in the long term
  • Events drive the market, and the news reports the events
  • Some news is fake
  • Competition is perfect
  • Supply and demand are capped
  • There is only one conflict occurring at a time
